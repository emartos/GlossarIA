# Code of Conduct

GlossarIA is an open, community-driven project.  
We want this space to remain respectful, constructive and welcoming.

By participating (Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions, etc.), you agree to:

---

## 1. Be respectful

- Treat others with kindness  
- Focus on ideas, not individuals  
- Accept that technical disagreements are normal  

---

## 2. Be inclusive

Do not engage in discriminatory, harassing or exclusionary behavior based on:

- gender  
- sexuality  
- disability  
- appearance  
- race or ethnicity  
- religion  
- nationality  

---

## 3. Use constructive communication

- Offer clear, actionable feedback  
- Ask questions if something is unclear  
- Avoid sarcasm in technical disagreement  

---

## 4. No spam or self-promotion abuse

Only share tools, links or resources when relevant and transparent.

---

## 5. Reporting issues

---

## 6. Enforcement

Maintainers may:

- Edit or remove comments, commits or PRs  
- Ban repeated offenders  
- Request changes in communication tone  

Our priority is maintaining a healthy and productive environment.
